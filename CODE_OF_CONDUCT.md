# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but be clear and direct.
- Focus feedback on ideas, code, and behavior, not on people.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or threats
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or sustained hostility
- Publishing private information without permission

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it through the repository or organization maintainers on GitHub. Please include links, screenshots, or other context that will help maintainers investigate.

Maintainers will review reports promptly and may remove comments, lock threads, or restrict participation as needed to protect the community.
